About Lone Survivors

Throughout each adventure in a level, you will be offered the opportunity to select from a variety of weaponry, magic, and traps to defeat your enemies. This games put you in control of a variety of different characters, each with their own active ability that sets them apart.

From the campaign mode, completing objectives to uncover the mystery of the island you are on, to an endless mode where you battle until the enemies overwhelm you to achieve the highest survival time, there is endless content to keep you engaged!

Unique Classes

6 unique classes, each with their own progression trees - play as a Barbarian, a Warrior, a Mage, or even a Necromancer!

Vast World

Four unique biomes, with eight different Locations within the Biomes, one for Endless and one for Survival each! Traverse through a forest, a deadly trap-filled dungeon, sandy island beach, and the fiery pits of a volcano!

Enemies and Bosses

Six Bosses to overcome, with plenty of Unique Elite enemies to encounter along the way!
